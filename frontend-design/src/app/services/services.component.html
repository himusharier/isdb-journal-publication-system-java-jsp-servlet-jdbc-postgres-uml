Our Services

Services for Readers

The publisher makes the articles free for all readers to download and read by open access policies. Even if any readers face difficulties in reading or downloading articles due to device incompatibility, they can request a full PDF version via email—sent as an attachment at no charge.

Readers are also welcome to submit complaints or objections regarding our content or published works. We handle such issues with respect and in a timely manner. Suggestions for improving our publishing quality are highly appreciated and warmly welcomed.

Services for Authors

We offer professional editing services for authors. Our language experts help fix grammar, syntax, and sentence structure to enhance readability. We also provide plagiarism checking and recommendations to avoid duplication.

If you’d like to refine your writing before submission, contact us to benefit from the expertise of our professional staff. Note: we do not support fraudulent articles or manipulated results. Our current services for authors include:

  • Manuscript proofreading and writing guidelines
  • Formatting manuscripts as per journal requirements
  • Referencing in specific styles
  • Grammar correction and error fixing
  • Plagiarism check with improvement suggestions

To get in touch for professional services, contact us.

Statement of Service Ethics

We aim to reduce plagiarism by checking similarities and offering editing services. However, we do not paraphrase or write academic papers from supplied data. Our role is to refine your manuscript's language, format, and style to meet publishing standards.

All communications regarding services—whether for readers or authors—will be handled only through our official publisher correspondent.
