Advancements in Neural Network Optimization Techniques

Authors: Jane Smith, John Doe, Emily Johnson

Publication Date: April 10, 2025

DOI: 10.1000/sample.2025.001

Abstract

This article explores recent advancements in neural network optimization, focusing on novel algorithms for improving training efficiency and model performance. We propose a hybrid approach combining gradient-based and evolutionary techniques, demonstrating significant improvements in convergence rates across benchmark datasets. The findings have implications for deep learning applications in computer vision and natural language processing.
